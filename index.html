Arithmetic Geometry in Chile

About the event

On December 2024 the event Arithmetic Geometry in Chile will be held at Santiago.

Langlands program aims to relate systems of polynomial equations with integer coefficients to automorphic forms, i.e. functions on symmetric spaces with a large number of discrete symmetries.

The focus of this event will be on some manifestations of this program, including:

  • Arithmetic Geometry
  • p-adic Variations

    • The program will include a Summer School addressed to PhD students and postdocs and a Conference:

  • Summer School (1st Week December 2024)
  • Conference (2nd Week December 2024).

    • Reseracher form all over the world will gather in Santiago. They will present their recent advances in mathematics.
