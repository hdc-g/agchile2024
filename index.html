About

On December 2024 the program Arithmetic and p-adic geometry in Chile will be held at Santiago:

  • Summer School (2-6th of December 2024)
  • Conference (9-13th of December 2024)

Langlands program aims to relate systems of polynomial equations with integer coefficients to automorphic forms, i.e. functions on symmetric spaces with a large number of discrete symmetries.

The focus of this event will be on some manifestations of this program, including:

  • p-adic Kudla program,
  • p-adic Langlands program,
  • p-adic L-functions and Iwasawa theory.

    • The program will include a Summer School addressed to students and young researchers and a Conference.

    Researcher form all over the world will gather in Santiago. They will present their recent advances in mathematics.
